Week 6 Lesson 2 - Reports & Analytics Setup
============================================

This lesson generates reports from Lesson 1 attendance data.

Prerequisites:
1. Complete Lesson 1 first (generate attendance.csv)
2. Have at least 2-3 attendance records

Check Lesson 1 output:
  Test-Path ..\lesson-1\output\attendance.csv

If no attendance data, run Lesson 1 first:
  cd ..\lesson-1
  python main.py
  # Do some check-ins/check-outs
  # Press 'q' to quit

Then run this lesson:
  cd ..\lesson-2
  python main.py

Reports will be saved to:
  output/reports/daily_YYYY-MM-DD.json
  output/reports/monthly_YYYY_MM.json
